1. Introduction

Welcome to Alarm App, developed by SmartHorizon ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our app works.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect (very minimal)
  • How we use that information
  • Your rights and choices
  • How third-party services we use handle data

By using Alarm App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

Key Point: Alarm App does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information that identifies you. All your alarm data is stored locally on your device.

2.1 Information You Provide

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Name
  • ❌ Email address
  • ❌ Phone number
  • ❌ User accounts or passwords
  • ❌ Social media profiles

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Alarm settings (time, label, repeat schedule)
  • Selected alarm sounds and ringtones
  • Wake-up challenge preferences (math, photo detection, typing, etc.)
  • Sleep sounds preferences
  • App settings and preferences
  • Usage statistics (alarm success rate, snooze frequency)

2.2 Location Information (Optional)

When: Only when you enable the weather feature for alarms

What: Your approximate location (neighborhood-level)

How: One-time location request when you enable weather

Purpose: To fetch current weather conditions from OpenWeather API

Storage: We do NOT store your coordinates. We only use your location to get the city name and request fresh weather data each time.

Control: You can deny location permission or disable weather features at any time in your device settings.

2.3 Photos and Camera Access (Optional)

When: Only when you use the Photo Detection wake-up challenge

What: Photos taken through the app's camera

How it works:

  • You select a predefined object (e.g., coffee mug, book, door)
  • When the alarm rings, you must take a photo of that object
  • The photo is processed entirely on your device
  • Photos never leave your device or get uploaded anywhere
  • Photos are stored in temporary app storage and automatically cleared by your device over time

Control: You can deny camera permission or not use this challenge type.

2.4 Device Storage Access (Optional)

When: Only when you choose to save analytics charts as images

What: Permission to save images to your device gallery

Purpose: To export your alarm statistics as shareable images

Control: This is entirely optional. You can deny storage permission.

2.5 Analytics Information

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to understand how users interact with our app and improve functionality. Firebase automatically collects:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Device language and region
  • General region information (such as country, derived from IP address)
  • App usage events (screens visited, features used)
  • Custom events we track:
    • Alarm created/deleted
    • Challenge completed/failed
    • Sleep sound played
    • Subscription events
  • Crash reports and technical diagnostics

Important: Firebase assigns an anonymous identifier to your device. We cannot identify you personally from this data.

Purpose: To improve app performance, fix bugs, and understand which features are most useful.

Note: We do NOT use Firebase data for advertising purposes. We do not show ads in our app.

2.6 Subscription Information

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. When you subscribe to premium features, RevenueCat collects:

  • Anonymous user ID (generated by RevenueCat)
  • Subscription status and purchase events
  • Device type and OS version
  • App version
  • Country/region (from App Store or Google Play Store)

Payment Information: We never see or store your payment details. All payments are processed by Apple (via App Store) or Google (via Play Store). Your credit card and billing information is handled entirely by them according to their privacy policies.

Purpose: To verify subscription status and provide premium features.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process certain limited personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The legal basis for processing analytics and diagnostic data is our Legitimate Interest as defined under Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR.

This processing is necessary to ensure application reliability, improve performance, monitor stability, and identify and fix technical issues.

We have conducted a Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA) to evaluate and balance our operational needs against the rights and freedoms of users. The assessment concluded that the impact on user privacy is minimal due to data minimization, the absence of direct identifiers, and the availability of opt-out controls provided at the operating system level.

4. Data Storage and Backup

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your alarm data is stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data.

4.2 Optional Cloud Backup

You can optionally back up your alarm data to your own cloud storage:

  • iOS: iCloud Drive (requires you to be signed into iCloud)
  • Android: Google Drive (requires you to sign into Google account through our app)

What gets backed up:

  • Alarm settings and configurations
  • Usage statistics (alarm success rates, challenge performance)
  • App preferences

How it works:

  • The app creates a backup file on your device
  • The file is uploaded directly to your iCloud or Google Drive account. Note that these backups are not separately encrypted by the app; they rely on the native security and encryption provided by Apple or Google.
  • We never see or access this data - it's stored in your personal cloud storage
  • You have full control to view, download, or delete these backups at any time
Important: Cloud backup is entirely optional and controlled by you. Your data in iCloud or Google Drive is subject to Apple's or Google's privacy policies, not ours. We recommend reviewing their policies:
  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local Storage: All alarm data is stored locally on your device, protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometrics)
  • Encryption: Communication with third-party services (Firebase, RevenueCat, OpenWeather) uses HTTPS encryption
  • No Central Database: We do not maintain a database of user information
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect what's necessary for app functionality
Your Responsibility: Since data is stored on your device, please protect your device with a passcode or biometric lock. If you enable cloud backup, secure your iCloud or Google account with a strong password and two-factor authentication.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Local Data

  • Retention: Data remains on your device for as long as you have the app installed
  • Deletion: When you uninstall the app, all local data is automatically deleted by your device's operating system
  • Manual Deletion: You cannot selectively delete data without uninstalling. To start fresh, you must uninstall and reinstall the app

7.2 Cloud Backup Data

  • Control: You have full control over backups in your iCloud or Google Drive
  • Deletion: You can delete backup files directly from your iCloud or Google Drive at any time
  • Retention: Backups remain in your cloud storage as long as you choose to keep them

7.3 Analytics Data

  • Firebase: Analytics data is retained by Google Firebase according to their data retention policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription data is retained according to RevenueCat's policy
  • Controller Limitation: We do not have the technical ability to delete individual analytics records, as data is aggregated and anonymized by the service provider.

8. Sharing Features

The app allows you to share alarm statistics with friends:

  • What you can share: Wake-up challenge scores, alarm consistency statistics, sleep duration data (in image format)
  • How it works: Statistics are exported as images on your device
  • Sharing method: You manually share images through your device's native share menu (Messages, WhatsApp, Instagram, etc.)
  • Our role: We only generate the image - we are not involved in the sharing process

Privacy tip: Be mindful of what you share publicly, as shared images may reveal your sleep patterns and alarm usage.

10. Your Rights and Choices

10.1 Data Access

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have direct access to your alarm data through the app interface.

10.2 Data Deletion

  • Local data: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Cloud backup: Delete backup files from your iCloud or Google Drive
  • Analytics: Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated by Firebase. We cannot identify individual users within analytics data. Where deletion is not technically feasible, data is retained according to Firebase’s retention controls and anonymization policies.

10.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Location: Disable weather features or deny location permission in device settings
  • Camera: Deny camera permission or don't use photo detection challenges
  • Cloud backup: Simply don't enable this feature
  • Analytics: We rely on legitimate interest to collect limited and anonymous analytics data for app improvement, stability, and bug fixing. You may object to this processing by using the privacy and analytics controls provided by your device’s operating system, which allow you to limit or disable the sharing of analytics and diagnostic data with app developers.

10.4 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we process (minimal, as explained above)
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (you control your local data)
  • Erasure: Request deletion (uninstall the app)
  • Data Portability: Export your data (use cloud backup feature)
  • Object to Processing: You may object to analytics processing by using the privacy and analytics controls provided by your device’s operating system.

Contact us at info@smarthorizon.co to exercise these rights.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal information

We do not discriminate against users who exercise their CCPA rights.
