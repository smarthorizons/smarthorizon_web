1. Introduction

Welcome to Prayer Timer, developed by Smart Horizon ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while helping you maintain your daily prayers.

Prayer Timer is a comprehensive Islamic prayer time application that calculates accurate prayer times based on your location, provides Qibla direction toward the Kaaba in Mecca, sends timely prayer notifications with customizable Adhan sounds, displays the Islamic (Hijri) calendar, and shows local weather at prayer times.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at admin@smarthorizon.co.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using Prayer Timer, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. What Information Do We Collect?

Key Point: Prayer Timer does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information that identifies you. All prayer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

We do NOT collect: name, email address, phone number, user accounts, passwords, or religious affiliation.

The following is stored locally on your device only:

  • Selected city name and its geographic coordinates
  • Prayer time calculation method preference
  • Custom prayer time adjustments (minutes per prayer)
  • Notification preferences (enabled prayers, selected Adhan sound)
  • App display settings (language, theme)
  • Hijri calendar date adjustment (if any)

2.2 Location and Internet Usage

Prayer Timer uses your location and an internet connection during setup to identify your city and apply the correct prayer time calculation method. Once your city is saved, prayer times, Qibla direction, and notifications all work offline. Internet is only needed again for weather or if you update your city.

Internet is required for:

  • City identification: When GPS is used, your coordinates are sent to a geocoding service to convert them into a city name
  • Country detection: Your country is identified to automatically apply the appropriate prayer time calculation method for your region (e.g., Muslim World League, ISNA for North America)
  • Manual city search: Searching for a city by name requires internet to query the location database
  • Weather data: Current weather at your location is fetched fresh from OpenWeather API each time you view it

Internet is NOT required for: prayer time display, prayer notifications, Qibla direction, or Hijri calendar — once your city is saved.

Two ways to set your location:

Option 1 — GPS Auto-Detection (requires location permission and internet)

  • The app requests your GPS location every time you open the app or bring it to the foreground
  • This ensures prayer times always reflect your current location — particularly useful if you travel between cities
  • Internet is used once to convert your coordinates into a city name and identify your country
  • Your city name and coordinates are saved locally on your device and reused for prayer time calculations, Qibla direction, and weather — without requesting location again for each feature

Option 2 — Manual City Search (requires internet for search only)

  • If you deny location permission, you can search for your city by name — this requires internet
  • Once selected, the city name and coordinates are saved locally and used identically to the GPS option for all features
  • Your saved city remains active on every launch — no GPS or internet needed for prayer times
  • Unlike GPS mode, the app will not automatically detect if you travel to another city; you can update your city manually at any time
  • If no internet is available but a city was previously saved, the app continues to work offline using the saved data

What we do NOT do:

  • ❌ Continuously track or monitor your movements
  • ❌ Send your location or coordinates to our servers
  • ❌ Share your location with any party other than the geocoding and weather services described in Section 5

2.3 Analytics Information

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to maintain app stability and improve reliability. Firebase anonymously collects device model and OS version, app version, device language and region (country-level), app usage events (screens visited, features used), and crash reports.

Firebase assigns an anonymous identifier to your device. We cannot identify you personally from this data. We do not track which specific prayers you perform or your religious practice habits.

Legal Basis: Analytics processing is based on our Legitimate Interest under GDPR Article 6(1)(f). See Section 3 for the full justification and your right to object.

2.4 Notification Data

Prayer time notifications are scheduled entirely on your device — no server is involved. The app calculates prayer times locally and creates local notifications. We do not track whether you interact with or dismiss notifications.

3. Legal Basis for Analytics Processing (GDPR)

We process anonymous analytics data based on our Legitimate Interest under Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR. We have conducted a Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA) and concluded the following:

Our Interest: Maintaining a stable and reliable prayer notification system that Muslims depend on for timely worship. Analytics helps us detect crashes, fix bugs, and ensure the app works reliably across thousands of device and OS combinations.

Impact on Users: Minimal — data is anonymous and aggregated, no personal identifiers are collected, no religious practices are tracked, and data is not used for advertising or profiling.

Your Right to Object (GDPR Article 21): You may object to analytics processing using your device's OS-level controls:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements → Turn off "Share iPhone Analytics" and "Share With App Developers"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Usage & Diagnostics → Turn off

These settings affect all apps on your device. This is the appropriate method for objecting to legitimate interest-based analytics under GDPR.

6. Data Storage and Security

All your prayer data, settings, and saved location are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area — protected by your device's operating system and accessible only by Prayer Timer. Communication with Firebase, the geocoding service, and OpenWeather uses HTTPS encryption. We do not maintain any central database of user information.

Your Responsibility: Since all personal data is stored on your device, please secure it with a strong passcode or biometric lock and keep your OS updated with security patches.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local data (prayer settings, saved city, coordinates) remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data automatically. On Android you can also clear app data via Settings → Apps → Prayer Timer → Storage → Clear Data.

Analytics data is retained by Google Firebase according to their data retention policy (typically 2–14 months), after which it is aggregated and anonymized permanently. Because this data is anonymous, we cannot identify or delete data belonging to a specific user.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.3 Data Deletion
