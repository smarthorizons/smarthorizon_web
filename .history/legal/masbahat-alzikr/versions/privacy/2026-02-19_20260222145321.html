1. Introduction

Welcome to Masbahat Al Zikr ("App"), developed by Smart Horizon ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while helping you maintain your spiritual practice of dhikr and tasbeeh.

Masbahat Al Zikr is a digital tasbeeh counter application that helps you count and track your dhikr (remembrance of Allah), manage custom and pre-set azkar, set notification reminders for daily azkar, and optionally back up your data to your personal cloud storage.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at admin@smarthorizon.co.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using Masbahat Al Zikr, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. What Information Do We Collect?

Key Point: Masbahat Al Zikr does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information that identifies you. All your tasbeeh counts, azkar, and settings are stored locally on your device.

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

We do NOT collect: name, email address (except for Google Sign-In if you choose Google Drive backup), phone number, user accounts, passwords, location data, network information, device contacts, camera access, or photos.

The following is stored locally on your device only:

  • Tasbeeh and dhikr counts and statistics
  • Custom azkar you create
  • Pre-set azkar you save or customize
  • Notification preferences and custom notification times
  • App settings and display preferences

2.2 Device Information (Limited Collection)

We collect device information (device model and OS version) only in the following specific situations:

  • When you submit a message through our in-app contact form to provide a suggestion or report a bug
  • When a crash occurs (sent automatically via Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix device-specific issues)

This information helps us understand which devices and OS versions may have compatibility issues.

2.3 Analytics Information

We use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance (by Google) to maintain app stability and improve reliability. These services anonymously collect:

  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics (which features are used), session duration and frequency, custom event names and parameters we define
  • Firebase Crashlytics: App crash reports and performance issues, device information included only when crashes occur
  • Firebase Performance: App performance metrics and technical performance data (loading times, response times)

Firebase assigns an anonymous identifier to your device. We cannot identify you personally from this data. We do not track the specific content of your dhikr or azkar, the specific phrases you recite, or your spiritual practice habits.

Legal Basis: Analytics processing is based on our Legitimate Interest under GDPR Article 6(1)(f). See Section 3 for full justification and your right to object.

2.4 Cloud Backup Data (Optional)

If you choose to enable the backup feature:

iOS Users: You can select between iCloud or Google Drive

  • iCloud: No sign-in required if iCloud is already activated on your device. Your app data is backed up to your personal iCloud storage. We do not have access to your iCloud account or data.
  • Google Drive: Requires Google Sign-In to access your Google Drive account. Your app data is backed up to your personal Google Drive storage. We do not have access to your Google Drive account or other files.

Android Users: Only Google Drive is available

  • Requires Google Sign-In to access your Google Drive account
  • We request only the permissions necessary to read and write backup files
  • Your app data is backed up to your personal Google Drive storage
  • We do not have access to your Google Drive account or other files

What gets backed up (both platforms):

  • All tasbeeh and dhikr counts and statistics
  • All custom azkar you created
  • Pre-set azkar you saved or customized
  • App settings and preferences
  • Notification settings

Backup options:

  • Manual backup: You can manually create a backup at any time
  • Automatic daily backup: Option to automatically back up when you open the app each day
  • No size limits on backups (limited only by your cloud account storage)
Important: Cloud backup is entirely optional. Your backup data is stored in your personal cloud account — we never see or access it. The data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy (for iCloud) or Google's Privacy Policy (for Google Drive), not ours.

2.5 Notification Data

All notifications are handled entirely locally on your device — no third-party notification services are used. You can customize notification times and preferences. Notification settings are stored only on your device. We do not track whether you interact with or dismiss notifications.

3. Legal Basis for Analytics Processing (GDPR)

We process anonymous analytics data based on our Legitimate Interest under Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR. We have conducted a Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA) and concluded the following:

Our Interest: Maintaining a stable and reliable spiritual tool that users depend on for daily dhikr practice. Analytics helps us detect crashes, fix bugs, and ensure the app works reliably across thousands of device and OS combinations.

Impact on Users: Minimal — data is anonymous and aggregated, no personal identifiers are collected, no specific dhikr content or spiritual practices are tracked, and data is not used for advertising or profiling.

Your Right to Object (GDPR Article 21): You may object to analytics processing using your device's OS-level controls:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements → Turn off "Share iPhone Analytics" and "Share With App Developers"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Usage & Diagnostics → Turn off

These settings affect all apps on your device. This is the appropriate method for objecting to legitimate interest-based analytics under GDPR.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Local Storage

All your tasbeeh data, azkar, and settings are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area — protected by your device's operating system and accessible only by Masbahat Al Zikr. No data is stored on our servers.

6.2 Cloud Backup Storage

If you enable cloud backup, your data is stored in your personal iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive account. We do not have access to your cloud storage or the data stored there. Your backup data is subject to the security measures and policies of Apple (iCloud) or Google (Google Drive).

6.3 Security Measures

Communication with Firebase services uses HTTPS encryption. We do not maintain any central database of user information. All tasbeeh data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up to your personal cloud storage.

Your Responsibility: Since all data is stored on your device, please secure it with a strong passcode or biometric lock. If you enable cloud backup, secure your iCloud or Google account with a strong password and two-factor authentication.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Local Data

Retention: Data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed.

Deletion: Uninstalling the app automatically deletes all local data. On Android, you can also clear app data via Settings → Apps → Masbahat Al Zikr → Storage → Clear Data.

7.2 Cloud Backup Data

Control: You have full control over backups in your iCloud or Google Drive.

Deletion: You can delete backup files directly from your cloud storage at any time.

Retention: Backups remain in your cloud storage as long as you choose to keep them.

7.3 Analytics Data

Analytics data is retained by Google Firebase according to their data retention policy (typically 2–14 months), after which it is aggregated and anonymized permanently. Because this data is anonymous, we cannot identify or delete data belonging to a specific user.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Data Access

Since all tasbeeh data is stored locally on your device, you have direct access through the app interface. You can view your counts, statistics, azkar, and settings at any time.

8.2 Data Deletion

  • Local data: Uninstall the app to delete all local data immediately
  • Cloud backup: Delete backup files from your iCloud or Google Drive
  • Analytics data: Cannot be individually deleted as it's anonymous and aggregated; future collection can be prevented via OS-level analytics controls

8.3 Analytics Opt-Out

You may object to analytics processing using your device OS settings as described in Section 3. This is the appropriate method under GDPR Article 21 for objecting to legitimate interest-based processing.

8.4 Cloud Backup Control

  • Enable or disable backup at any time in app settings
  • Switch between manual and automatic backup modes
  • Switch between iCloud and Google Drive (iOS only)
  • Revoke Google Drive access by disconnecting your Google account in app settings
  • Delete backup files directly from your cloud storage

8.5 Notification Control

You can disable or customize notifications:

  • iOS: Settings → Notifications → Masbahat Al Zikr
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Masbahat Al Zikr → Notifications
