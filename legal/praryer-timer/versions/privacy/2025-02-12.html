Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2025

Quick Summary: Prayer Timer collects location data to provide accurate prayer times for your area. We use Firebase Analytics to improve app functionality. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Introduction

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Smart Horizon ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us at smarthorizonoman@gmail.com.

This privacy policy describes how we might use your information if you:

  • Download and use our mobile application — Prayer Timer
  • Engage with us in other related ways, including any sales, marketing, or events

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If there are any terms in this privacy policy that you do not agree with, please discontinue use of our app immediately.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

1.1 Personal Information You Disclose to Us

In Short: We collect minimal personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities in the app, or when you contact us.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

In Short: Some information — such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address and device characteristics — is collected automatically when you use our app.

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the app. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App features used, screens viewed, time stamps, crash data
  • IP Address: Your device's IP address for general location detection

1.3 Location Information

In Short: We collect location data to provide accurate prayer times for your area.

Why We Collect Location Data:

  • Prayer Times Calculation: Your precise location is required to calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic coordinates
  • Qibla Direction: To show the correct direction to Mecca for prayer
  • Mosque Finder: To show nearby mosques in your area (if feature is enabled)

We may request access to location-based information from your mobile device. You can manage location permissions in your device settings. Note: If you deny location access, core features like prayer time calculation will not work.

1.4 Analytics Information

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to understand how users interact with our app. Firebase automatically collects:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Device language and region
  • General location (country/city level)
  • App usage events (screens visited, features used)
  • Crash reports and technical diagnostics

Important: Firebase assigns an anonymous identifier to your device. We cannot identify you personally from this data.

Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable analytics collection at any time from the app's Settings screen under "Privacy Settings."

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

In Short: We use your information to provide and improve Prayer Timer's services.

We use the information we collect or receive:

  • To deliver prayer times: Calculate accurate prayer schedules based on your location
  • To send notifications: Prayer time reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • To improve our app: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and add features
  • To ensure security: Monitor for technical issues and maintain app stability
  • To comply with legal obligations: Respond to legal requests and prevent illegal activities

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

In Short: We only share information with service providers necessary to operate the app, or to comply with laws.

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your data in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for analytics and crash reporting
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit permission

3.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Usage analytics and crash reporting (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services / Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

In Short: We keep your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy.

We will retain your personal information for as long as you use the app. When you uninstall Prayer Timer, all locally stored data (prayer times, settings, preferences) is deleted from your device.

Analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's data retention policies (typically 2-14 months, depending on settings).

5. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

In Short: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards, no electronic transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Security measures include:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure data storage on your device
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited data collection (only what's necessary)

6. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

In Short: You have rights regarding your personal data, including access, correction, and deletion.

6.1 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics collection in app settings
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at smarthorizonoman@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

6.3 Location Permissions

You can manage location permissions at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Prayer Timer
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Prayer Timer → Permissions → Location

6.4 Notification Permissions

You can disable prayer time notifications:

  • iOS: Settings → Notifications → Prayer Timer
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Prayer Timer → Notifications

6.5 Analytics Opt-Out

Disable analytics collection in the app:

  • Open Prayer Timer → Settings → Privacy Settings → Disable "Help Improve the App"

9. Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") feature. Currently, no uniform technology standard for recognizing DNT signals has been finalized.

As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals. If a standard for online tracking is adopted in the future, we will update this privacy policy accordingly.
